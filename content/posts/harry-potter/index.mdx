Went to a bar in Sydney with a few friends recently since it was holding a spicy event where they decorated the entire venue with spooky props for Halloween and held Harry Potter potion classes that got you to solve riddles for ingredients to make cocktails. We originally booked thirteen tickets, but only seven people signed up, so the rest went to waste except for one, which was given to another friend of ours that was coincidentally coming back from class when we were just heading out and ended up getting dragged along with us. We were supposed to dress up for the event, but I didn't have any related costumes, so a friend gave me his wizard hat that he took from the dining hall, which had spare Halloween props lying around from the Halloween dinner hosted a couple of days before. So massive shoutout to my boi Daniel, it wouldn't have been the same experience without the wizard hat.

In terms of the actual event, it was kinda like a scavenger hunt where we had to go around the bar and look for certain items and clues, which was pretty entertaining. There would always be a bottle to be collected at the end of every riddle, which most likely just contained alcohol. We would then pour these colored bottles into a big ass cauldron, at the very end adding dry ice into it to create some smoke for extra swag 👈😎👈, before pouring it out into cups for everyone to drink. Everyone had about two cups to drink, and the drinks tasted to be around 15% alcohol as well, so overall it was a good sesh.
