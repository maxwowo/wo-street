Aight I've just spent the entire day doing random ass maths questions for an exam on Monday. Still several weeks behind, still locked down, whatever. Here's a review of some of the karaoke bars in Auckland.

## Tanghui Karaoke Bar

- **Normal price**: IDK probably pretty expensive ey
- **Happy hours price**: $25 per person with food
- **Happy hours duration**: 6pm-10pm

Decent place, cheap with pretty gucci food. Quite hard to find though and the parking lot is small AF. It's so small that people might park behind you and you'll have to call them out. Pretty good song selection, a lot of C-pop and J-rock songs. No lesser-known bands like Thousand Foot Krutch and Starset though 😔. They probably download their songs from Youtube since even if you completely disable sing-along, you can still hear some of the lyrics. They're also the cheapest option out of the three. I'm actually convinced that they are a front for money laundering since they have to be running at a loss with that pricing.

## Jinzun Karaoke Bar

- **Normal price**: IDK probably pretty expensive ey
- **Happy hours price**: $30 per person with food
- **Happy hours duration**: 6pm-10pm

We've only hit up Jinzun once and it was a pretty average experience. Both the rooms and the food portions are smaller compared to Tanghui and they charge more as well. They also have a smaller song selection but all the songs have higher quality. That's probably because they actually use legit tracks instead of just downloading videos from Youtube and then removing the vocals like Tanghui does. But yeah would not recommend. We only went there because NZ just got out of lock down and only Jinzun was open at that time. Beggars can't be choosers.

## Seong Oh

- **Normal price**:
  - < 5 people: $30 per hour per room
  - < 7 people: $40 per hour per room

I don't actually know what the bar is called or what the owner's name is. We've just been calling him Seong Oh because he looks like a dude we know that goes by that name, so might as well just call him that ey. He's pretty cool though with a 🔥 haircut, and if you compliment his hairstyle he'll give you extra time. But he always gives us extra time anyway because we were his first customers so we're basically his bois. He's also the closest out of all three options so we visit him pretty frequently. I wonder if his shop will survive the lock down this time.
