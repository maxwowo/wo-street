I was originally gonna title this post "spa review," but then I realized it'll be more entertaining to talk about some dead skin cells instead, so you're gonna have to bear with me on this one.

So a couple weeks ago the gang and I drove to a place in NZ famous for its hot spring spas called Rotorua. Fun fact about the place, its spas are so full of minerals that the entire town constantly smells like shit, which is a pretty unfortunate side effect. Had to wake up really early to pick up the others but it wasn't too bad to drive there since I got to blast rock music, which was so fast paced that it made the drive feel shorter. We also stopped by an ice cream shop on the way there, which was absolutely necessary because apparently they make the best ice cream in all of NZ. The ice cream tasted just like ice cream, nothing special, so it gets a 8/10.
