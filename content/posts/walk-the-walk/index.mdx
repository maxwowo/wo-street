Went out with the boys today for a pretty spicy hike. Weather wasn't too bad, but could've been sunnier, even brought my precious SPF 50+ sunscreen to the walk, which ended up not being used because it was just cloudy all the way. Couldn't find a parking spot initially since every spot had a two-hour limit, but we eventually found somewhere that was parkable, so all was well.

We started somewhere in the North Sydney Wharf, played around in a playground for a bit to wait for a friend that was parking, then started the hike. After walking around some streets, checking out some mansions, watching Matthew pole dance, and taking my first FB profile picture in ages, we went to a ferry stop to go back to civilization. At that point it was starting to fog up, and the platform was also creaking, which made sounds very similar to the scary audio they play in horror movies, so we just did the big five personality test to distract ourselves while we waited for the ferry.

![Personality](./personality.png)

For some reason I got in the 4<sup>th</sup> percentile for agreeableness, which didn't sound right, so I did the test again with more extreme answers and ended up getting in the 1<sup>st</sup> percentile. That's some bullshit, I don't agree with that at all.

![Again](./personality_again.png)

Had some Thai food for dinner, which was pretty good but also spicy AF. I'm gonna feel it tomorrow.
